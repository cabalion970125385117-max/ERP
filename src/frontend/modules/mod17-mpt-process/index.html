NADCAP AC7114 — MPT 6-Step Process
Step 1 — Pre-Cleaning
Remove all contaminants, grease, scale and coatings that may mask indications. Solvent or alkaline cleaning methods. Verify cleanliness before proceeding.
Step 2 — Equipment Setup & Verification
Verify UV lamp intensity ≥1000 fc at 15 inches. Ambient light ≤2 fc. Equipment calibration within tolerance. Record all values.
Step 3 — Magnetisation
Apply required current (AC/HWDC/FWDC) per procedure. Verify field strength with pie gauge or Hall effect gauss meter. Record amperage and field strength.
Step 4 — Particle Application
Apply fluorescent wet particle bath. Verify bath concentration 0.1–0.4 mL/100 mL per ASTM centrifuge tube. Record concentration and particle brand.
Step 5 — Examination & Interpretation
Examine all surfaces under UV light. Apply 10× overlap technique. Record any indications, locations and sizes. Apply acceptance/rejection criteria per customer spec.
Step 6 — Demagnetisation & Post-Cleaning
Demagnetise to ≤3 gauss residual field. Verify with gauss meter. Perform post-cleaning as required by customer specification.
